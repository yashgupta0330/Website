User Experience Impacted by Complex and Unfocused CRM

Workflow Automation Complexity: Building and maintaining automation for lead nurturing, ticket routing, or follow-ups can be complex and time-consuming. Even minor process changes often require reconfiguration, adding to operational overhead.

Data Overload Without Visual Clarity: Too much raw data is shown without prioritization or visualization, making important KPIs are often buried under less relevant metrics.

User Adoption & Training: The UI is cluttered with too many features, making key actions hard to find. A lack of visual hierarchy and intuitive layout further hinders usability.

Lack of Role-Based Design: The same interface is used across sales reps, managers, and support agents, as a result, role-specific features are not prioritized.
