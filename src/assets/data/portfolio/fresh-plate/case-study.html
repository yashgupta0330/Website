A Consumer Food App Designed for Everyday Meal Planning

We partnered with Fresh Plate to design a modern and intuitive food app tailored for daily use by a diverse user base. The platform combines multiple features—meal planning, community engagement, profile settings, shopping lists, and more—into a single, seamless experience. Our goal was to simplify how users plan meals, discover content, and manage their preferences. Designed with accessibility and clarity in mind, Fresh Plate is built to offer a reliable and enjoyable food experience.

Designing a Scalable Food App for Everyday Users With Diverse Needs

Diverse User Roles: From customers and nutrition-focused users to admin teams and field staff, each user group needed a streamlined, role-specific interface without added complexity.

Low-End Device & Network Constraints: Many users accessed the app via shared or older smartphones, often in low-connectivity zones—requiring an interface that loads fast and works seamlessly.

Disjointed Workflows: Features like meal planning, complaint resolution, and shopping lists were siloed. We had to unify them into a single, intuitive app experience

Consistency Across Modules: Maintaining a cohesive visual and interaction system was critical for usability and scalability.

Empowering Technicians with a Custom Mobile App

Technicians operate in dynamic, on-the-go environments where timely response and accurate service are critical. A custom-built mobile app enhances their productivity by offering real-time access to task lists, service histories, manuals, and digital checklists—all from the field.

The app also enables instant communication, status updates, and media uploads from job sites, ensuring transparency and better issue resolution.
