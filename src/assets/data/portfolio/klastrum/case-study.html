A Smart Classroom Platform Built for Modern Learning Environments

Digital Live Services approached us to design a unified platform that empowers teachers and students with interactive classroom experiences. The goal was to seamlessly integrate various educational content types—e-books, videos, quizzes, puzzles—into one intuitive interface usable across smartboards, tablets, and desktops.
