Overview

Take and track notes directly inside outlook

Finc is a smart note-taking add-in for Outlook that simplifies the way users capture, organize, and retrieve contextual information linked to emails and contacts. Built to reduce workflow interruptions, Finc enables seamless note creation directly within Outlook — supporting productivity, collaboration, and information retention.

Challenges

Solving Email Management with Simplicity and Context

  • Seamless Integration: Outlook 16 introduced changes to core functionalities, prompting us to research and craft a UX that works seamlessly across desktop, cloud, Windows, and macOS.
  • Context Preservation: Notes needed to connect fluidly to both emails and contacts. Structuring this relationship while keeping it intuitive posed a significant UX challenge.
  • Visual & Functional Clarity: With features like related threads, filtering, and recovery, we had to keep the interface clean and easy to use—even for first-time users.

Purpose and Direction

Finc improves note-taking in Outlook, focusing on simplicity for user adoption.

Our aim was to design a seamless note-taking experience that lives inside your inbox—no switching tabs, no added friction. By grounding the interface in familiarity and context, we wanted users to easily capture and revisit insights without breaking their workflow.
